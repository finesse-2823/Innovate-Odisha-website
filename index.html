F.A.Q

  • What is a Hackathon?

    A hackathon is social coding event where programmers, designers and developers collaborate to solve a problem and compete for cash prizes. It’s one part party, one part work-your-butt-off overnight battle against the clock and the competition.

  • Who can participate?

    A hackathon is open to anybody interested in technology and provides opportunities to learn, build, and contribute.

  • Can beginners participate?

    Come learn and have fun for your first hackathon at Innovate Odisha.

  • Is there any fees for the hackathon?

    No. You are not required to make any payments to anyone. It is entirely free to all.

  • Can we form team?

    Yes, it is required that each participant be a part of a team. Participants are required to form a team of 4 members from the same institution/university.Every squad is required to include at least one female player. Each participant must belong to a single team and submit a single project.

  • Have more questions?

    Join our Telegram Community Get in touch with a bigger community and ask any further queries you may have. Looking forward to welcoming you.
